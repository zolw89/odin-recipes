Burger

It attacks your taste buds! Spicy chorizo-style sausage and lean ground turkey combine to make a kick-butt burger that's full of taste but won't leave you feeling stuffed. This burger could also be grilled outdoors for a smokier flavor. Goes great with a michel ada or frozen margarita.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground turkey
  • 4 links Mexican chorizo sausage, casings removed
  • 2 tablespoons buttery spread
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Â¼ cup mayonnaise, divided

Steps

  1. Step 1

    2. Combine ground turkey, chorizo, garlic, onion powder, chipotle flakes, and black pepper in a large bowl; mix with your hands until well-blended. Shape into 4 loosely-packed patties.

  2. Step 2

    3. Heat a griddle or large skillet over medium heat until hot; grease with buttery spread. Place patties on the griddle; cook until browned, about 3 minutes. Reduce heat to medium-low; continue cooking for about 5 minutes. Flip patties and cook until browned on the second side, about 6 minutes.

  3. Step 3

    4. Top each patty with a slice of pepperjack cheese. Cook until cheese is melted, about 1 minute more.
